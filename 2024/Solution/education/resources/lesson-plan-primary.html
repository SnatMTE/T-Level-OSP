Primary Lesson Plan — Animals & Habitats

Age range: 7–11 | Duration: 60 minutes

Learning objectives

  • Understand how animal adaptations help them survive
  • Identify basic habitats (forest, wetlands, savanna)
  • Describe food chains and food webs

Activity

Start with a short talk, then split into groups for the matching game, end with a quiz to check understanding.
